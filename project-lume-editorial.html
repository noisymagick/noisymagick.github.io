The Challenge

Lume Magazine, a publication focused on sustainable fashion and lifestyle, needed a complete editorial redesign to refresh their visual identity. The challenge was to create a layout system that could accommodate diverse content types while maintaining a cohesive, sophisticated aesthetic.

The Solution

I developed a flexible editorial system with modular layouts, distinctive typography pairings, and a refined color palette. The design celebrates visual storytelling while ensuring readability and creating space for impactful photography.
