The Challenge

Verdant Health approached me to design a mobile application that helps users track their daily nutrition and make healthier food choices. The app needed to be intuitive, engaging, and provide personalized recommendations based on user preferences.

The Solution

I created a user-friendly mobile app with features like meal tracking, recipe suggestions, and progress analytics. The interface uses a clean, nature-inspired design language that aligns with the brand's commitment to wellness and sustainability.
