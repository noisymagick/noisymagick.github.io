Brand Identity

About the Project

Developed a comprehensive brand identity for a modern Scandinavian restaurant, including logo creation and brand guidelines. The brand needed to reflect Nordic culinary traditions while appealing to a contemporary audience.

Responsibilities

Researched the restaurant's values and target audience, designed the logo and visual identity system, created brand guidelines document, and developed collateral materials including menus, stationery, and signage.

Technologies Used

Adobe Illustrator for logo design and vector graphics, Figma for brand guidelines layout, and collaboration with print vendors for production.

Challenges Overcome

Balanced traditional Nordic aesthetics with modern design trends, created a versatile logo system that works across various applications, and developed a cohesive color palette that evokes the restaurant's atmosphere.

Outcome

A complete brand identity that successfully communicates the restaurant's essence. The client reported increased brand recognition and positive customer feedback on the visual identity.
