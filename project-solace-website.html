Website Design

The Challenge

Solace Mental Health needed a compassionate and professional website to showcase their therapy services. The design had to convey trust, warmth, and accessibility while maintaining a professional appearance for healthcare services.

The Solution

I designed a calming, user-centered website with easy navigation, accessible content, and warm color accents. The site includes service pages, therapist profiles, and an appointment booking system, all designed to reduce anxiety and build trust with potential clients.
